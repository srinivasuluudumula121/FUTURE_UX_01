# Business Research

## Business Name

Partha Dental Clinic

## Business Type

Dental Healthcare Service Website Redesign

## Project Goal

The goal of this redesign project was to improve website clarity, trust, and lead generation for first-time visitors.

The redesigned experience focuses on:

* Better service communication
* Higher appointment conversion
* Improved trust building
* Clearer user navigation
* Better mobile responsiveness

## Current Website Problems

The existing website presented several usability and conversion issues:

* Unclear visual hierarchy
* Weak call-to-action placement
* Information-heavy sections
* Poor content scanning
* Limited emotional trust-building
* Inconsistent spacing and structure

These issues may reduce appointment bookings and increase user drop-off.

## Redesign Objective

The redesign focused on building a modern, professional, and high-conversion experience inspired by premium healthcare websites while maintaining Partha Dental Clinic’s original content and brand identity.

## Key Focus Areas

* Service clarity
* Better visual hierarchy
* Conversion-focused homepage
* Appointment optimization
* Trust-building sections
* Mobile responsiveness

# Competitor Analysis

Competitor websites and modern healthcare experiences were studied to understand:

* Trust-building patterns
* CTA placement
* Homepage hierarchy
* Conversion strategies
* Mobile responsiveness

## Design Inspiration

The redesign was visually inspired by premium modern healthcare websites with:

* Rounded glass-like buttons
* Floating information cards
* Minimal layouts
* Clean spacing
* Premium typography
* Strong visual hierarchy

The goal was not to copy layouts, but to adapt modern UX patterns to Partha Dental Clinic’s brand, content, and service offering.

## Improvement Opportunities Identified

The redesign improves:

* Homepage clarity
* Service discoverability
* Appointment flow
* Trust communication
* Lead generation effectiveness

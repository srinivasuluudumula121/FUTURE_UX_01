# Target Users

## Primary Users

### Families

Parents searching for reliable dental care for children and family members.

Goals:

* Trustworthy clinic
* Safe treatment
* Easy appointment booking

### Working Professionals

Busy individuals seeking quick dental appointments.

Goals:

* Fast booking
* Convenient scheduling
* Trusted specialists

### First-Time Patients

Visitors unfamiliar with the clinic.

Goals:

* Understand services
* Build trust
* See reviews and credentials
* Book confidently

## User Pain Points

Users often struggle with:

* Fear of treatment
* Unclear pricing or process
* Trust concerns
* Information overload
* Difficulty finding appointment details

## User Intent

Most visitors arrive with one of these goals:

* Learn about treatment
* Book appointment
* Compare clinics
* Check credibility
* Contact clinic quickly

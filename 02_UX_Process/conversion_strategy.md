# Conversion Strategy

The redesign focused on improving website conversion by guiding visitors toward appointment booking and inquiry actions.

## Conversion Improvements

### Strong Hero Section

A clear value proposition was placed above the fold to immediately explain clinic value and encourage action.

### Better CTA Placement

Appointment booking buttons were strategically placed throughout the website to reduce drop-off.

### Trust Building

The redesign introduced:

* Testimonials
* Credentials
* Experience indicators
* Patient reassurance content

### Reduced Friction

Content hierarchy was simplified to make information easier to scan and understand.

### Mobile Optimization

Sticky mobile-friendly CTA patterns were considered to improve lead generation on smaller devices.

## Expected UX Improvements

The redesign aims to improve:

* User trust
* Website clarity
* Lead generation
* Appointment inquiries
* Visitor retention

# Information Architecture

## Website Structure

The redesigned Partha Dental Clinic website was structured to improve clarity, trust, and appointment conversion.

The information hierarchy was intentionally simplified to help users quickly understand services and take action.

## Main Website Navigation

### Home

Clinic overview, trust building, treatments, testimonials, and appointment CTA.

### Services

Detailed treatment information and service explanations.

### Contact / Appointment

Lead generation page focused on consultation booking and communication.

## Homepage Structure

Navbar
→ Hero Section
→ Trust Indicators
→ Services Preview
→ Why Choose Us
→ Treatment Categories
→ Testimonials
→ FAQ
→ Appointment CTA
→ Footer

## Design Goal

The structure prioritizes:

* Faster understanding
* Better scanning
* Trust building
* Higher appointment conversion
* Reduced friction

# Wireframing Process

Low-fidelity wireframes were created before visual design to focus on layout clarity and conversion flow.

The wireframes prioritized:

* Clear content hierarchy
* Better CTA visibility
* Reduced visual clutter
* Strong trust-building sections
* Mobile responsiveness

## Pages Explored

### Homepage

Conversion-focused homepage redesign.

### Service Detail Page

Detailed treatment explanation and benefits.

### Contact / Appointment Page

Simple lead generation and contact experience.

## Design Focus

The layout was intentionally structured to improve:

* Readability
* User trust
* Appointment conversion
* Information scanning

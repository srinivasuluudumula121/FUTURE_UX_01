## Prototype Goal

The interactive prototype was created to simulate a realistic user journey through the redesigned **Partha Dental Clinic website**.

The experience focuses on:

- Homepage exploration
- Service discovery
- Appointment conversion
- Contact interaction
- Responsive website behavior

---

## Prototype Experience

Users can explore:

### Homepage
- Service overview
- Trust-building sections
- CTA interactions
- Navigation flow

### Service Detail Page
- Treatment explanation
- Benefits and process
- Service information hierarchy
- Appointment CTA

### Contact / Appointment Page
- Appointment form
- Contact flow
- Clinic information
- Lead generation experience

---

## UX Goal

The prototype was designed to test:

- Conversion clarity
- User navigation
- CTA visibility
- Trust communication
- Mobile responsiveness

The interaction flow was intentionally designed to feel professional, minimal, and conversion-focused.
